## 💡 Common User Questions

**Q: Is this medical advice?**  
A: No. Informational only. Consult healthcare providers.

**Q: Can I use this for insulin dosing?**  
A: No. Never use for medication dosing. Follow your healthcare provider's guidance.

**Q: Are calculations accurate?**  
A: They're estimates. Individual responses vary. Monitor blood glucose.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes. Local storage only, no tracking, no health data collected.

**Q: How often is it updated?**  
A: Cached 30 days. Searches 2023-2025 sources. Shows "last updated" date.
